Tuned for the next generation of prenatal care

NIPTunegx® brings together cell-free DNA science, accredited laboratory operations and clinician-first reporting — to make non-invasive prenatal testing accessible, accurate and clear.

Our mission

Clinical-grade screening every family can rely on

We exist so that no family has to choose between answers and safety. By combining whole-genome cell-free DNA analysis with first-trimester risk modeling, NIPTunegx® equips clinicians with reports that drive better-informed conversations and more confident decisions.

Our test is performed by certified and accredited laboratories according to international and local standards — because behind every report is a clinician, a parent and a future being shaped.

What we believe

  • Screening should be safe — never invasive when it doesn't need to be.
  • Reports should be readable — by clinicians and the families they serve.
  • Quality is non-negotiable — accreditation, validation, transparency.
  • Access matters — language, geography and clinic size shouldn't limit care.

Quality you can audit

Each NIPTunegx® report is traceable end-to-end — from sample receipt through sequencing, bioinformatics and clinical interpretation.
