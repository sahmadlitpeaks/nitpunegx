Choose the right NIPT panel for your patient

From common aneuploidies to a comprehensive whole-genome screen — pick the panel that fits the clinical indication, then jump to the side-by-side comparison.

NIPTune C NIPTune S NIPTune S+ NIPTune X NIPTune X+ Compare all
Essential

NIPTune C

Screening for the five most clinically common chromosomal aneuploidies — including Down, Edwards, Patau, and the sex-chromosome aneuploidies.

Request NIPTune C

Conditions screened

  • · Trisomy 21 (Down)
  • · Trisomy 18 (Edwards)
  • · Trisomy 13 (Patau)
  • · Turner syndrome (45,X)
  • · Trisomy X (47,XXX)
  • · Klinefelter (47,XXY)
  • · Jacobs (47,XYY)
  • · Optional fetal sex
Best fit: routine prenatal screening when the patient has no specific high-risk indications.
Extended

NIPTune S

Everything in NIPTune C, plus all autosomal trisomies and whole-genome monosomies — broader chromosomal coverage for indicated patients.

Request NIPTune S

What it adds over NIPTune C

  • · All autosomal trisomies (genome-wide)
  • · Whole-genome monosomies
  • · Rare aneuploidies
  • · Optional fetal sex
Best fit: ultrasound abnormalities, prior abnormal screening, or family history without a specific microdeletion concern.
Recommended

NIPTune S+

All aneuploidies plus the most clinically common microdeletion / duplication (CNV) syndromes — the most popular choice for advanced screening.

Request NIPTune S+

Microdeletion / duplication syndromes included

  • · DiGeorge syndrome (22q11.2)
  • · Cri-du-chat (5p−)
  • · 1p36 deletion
  • · Wolf-Hirschhorn (4p−)
  • · Prader-Willi / Angelman (15q11)
  • · Pallister-Killian syndrome
  • · Jacobsen syndrome
  • · Langer-Giedion syndrome
Best fit: advanced maternal age, abnormal FTCT, ultrasound markers, or general request for a more comprehensive screen.
Comprehensive

NIPTune X

Genome-wide cell-free DNA analysis with extended structural variant detection — broader CNV coverage than S+.

Request NIPTune X

What it adds over NIPTune S+

  • · Extended CNV panel (≥ 7 Mb)
  • · Whole-genome resolution
  • · Additional rare microdeletion syndromes
  • · Detailed individualized report
Best fit: high-risk patients where broader structural-variant detection is clinically warranted.
Premium

NIPTune X+

Our most comprehensive panel — combines the full NIPTune X content with single-gene disorder analysis and priority genetic counseling.

Request NIPTune X+

Additional inclusions

  • · Single-gene disorder panel
  • · Skeletal dysplasia screening
  • · RASopathies screening
  • · Priority genetic counseling included
  • · Expedited turnaround option
Best fit: complex cases, suspicious ultrasound findings, or families with known monogenic risk.
