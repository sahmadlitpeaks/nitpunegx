What happens next

  1. Within 1 business day: medical affairs confirms the request and shipping address.
  2. Kit dispatch: blood-collection tubes and requisition arrive at your clinic.
  3. Sample drawn: 10 mL maternal blood, courier collected.
  4. Report delivered: 3–5 working days via the clinician portal.
